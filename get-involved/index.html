There are three ways you can help Saajha.

Financial Support

Saajha per school per month cost is about INR 600 for Saajhedar schools and INR 8,000 per school per month for impact schools. We are majorly a grant-based organization and hence depend on philanthropic organizations and individuals to meet our expenses. In case you are interested in supporting us, please fill this form
