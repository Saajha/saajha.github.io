Working with 1000+ schools and School Management Committees impacting 1 million+ children in Government schools of Delhi

Enhancing parental and community engagement in learning of children by capacity building of the School Management Committees

Building leadership in schools for greater collaboration between the schools and the community

Providing on-ground support by community engagement activities to raise awareness about learning of children

About half the children in Government schools from class 5th cannot read text of class 2nd. Lack of parental and community engagement in learning of children is a key reason for poor learning outcomes in public primary schools. Saajha works towards enhancing parental and community engagement in Government schools, thereby improving learning of children.

Who we are

Saajha is a not-for-profit based out of Delhi and works with public primary schools to enhance parental and community engagement
