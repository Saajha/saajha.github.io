Our Journey

2012 - The Idea

  • Idea behind Saajha was conceived in 2012 by Abhishek Choudhary, and Saransh Vaswani, two Gandhi fellows, and class-mates at Hansraj College, Delhi.
  • The idea was supported by the Central Square Foundation.

2013 - Project Saajha

  • The project commenced in 57 schools in North and East Delhi Municipal Corporation.
  • Funding support was provided by Central Square Foundation.

2014 - Saajha

  • In 2014, Saajha was registered as a not-for-profit company.
  • Saajha co-founders receive Echoing Green Fellowship.
  • 30 additional schools under South Delhi Municipal Corporation were covered under its operations.
  • Saajha has entered into a partnership with the Government of Delhi to scale its interventions to 1,000 schools.
