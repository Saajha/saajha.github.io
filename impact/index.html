Impact

Saajha’s impact has been recorded on two parameters

Building leadership capabilities of SMCs

Improvement in leadership capabilities is measured by a 5 X 4 growth rubric, which classifies leadership skills into 5 levels (Zero, Formed, Involved, Functional and Strong) on 4 parameters – SMC meetings, Parent Teacher meeting and overall learning environment and involvement in School development Plan. Significant improvements have been observed on the growth rubric for the schools we have been operational in.

Building leadership qualities is likely to translate into better learning outcomes for children in Government schools. Saajha has assessed the improvement in learning levels through an internal sample-based study.
