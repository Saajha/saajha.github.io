Vision and Mission

Saajha's Vision

Saajha aims to build a national level peer engagement platform of 100,000 School Management Committees (SMCs) across the country. This national level network would span 8 states and 200 districts, and would benefit more than 150 million children studying in public primary schools in the country.
This would also enable collaboration among the SMCs at national level, which would allow them to share learning experiences from across the country, and therefore, ensuring sustainability of the intervention. The platform would also give the SMCs sufficient leverage to push for systemic and instutional reforms for facilitating regulatory support to community engagement.
